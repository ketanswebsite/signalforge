Common Issues & Solutions

  • Incorrect Card Details
    Double-check your card number, expiry date, and CVV code. Make sure all details match your card exactly.
  • Insufficient Funds
    Ensure your card has sufficient funds or available credit limit to cover the subscription amount.
  • Card Declined by Bank
    Your bank may have declined the transaction for security reasons. Contact your bank to authorize the payment.
  • International Payments Blocked
    Some cards don't support international transactions. Enable international payments or use a different card.
  • Expired or Invalid Card
    Check that your card hasn't expired and is still active. Try using a different payment method if available.
